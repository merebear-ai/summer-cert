At a Glance

Learning by doing — concept to practice in the same hour

A six-week, cohort-based experience that prepares educators to leverage and embed AI into teaching and learning workflows. Each session is grounded in your real teaching context.

Format

Live virtual workshops

A mix of teacher-directed instruction, group collaboration, and independent learning — one hour per week.

Audience

Faculty & instructional staff

Open to educators at BoodleBox Higher Education partner institutions.

Outcome

Certificate of Mastery

In BoodleBox AI-Integrated Teaching and Learning, awarded on completion of all six micro-credentials.

Weekly Structure

Six weeks, six core competencies

Each week builds a core competency in AI-integrated teaching and learning — with a practical application component and targeted assistance throughout, put into practice inside BoodleBox.

Week
01

AI-Integrated Learning Fundamentals & an Introduction to BoodleBox

Faculty learn to recognize where AI-integrated assignment opportunities exist across their own courses — where AI can meaningfully strengthen learning — alongside the basics of BoodleBox: key terminology, setting up accounts, and platform navigation.

Week
02

Simulation / TA Bot Creation

Custom bot building breakdown and best practices, plus hands-on creation of custom bots that act as teaching assistants or domain simulators. Focus on persona design, knowledge grounding, guardrails, and iterative testing.

Week
03

BoodleBox Classroom: AI-Integrated Assignments

Translating course learning goals into AI-supported assignments. Faculty design at least one assignment that pairs traditional outcomes with structured AI use, including scaffolding and academic-integrity guardrails.

Week
04

AI-Integrated Group Collaboration

Differentiating between — and blending — independent and collaborative learning tools; folder system best practices; managing shared workspaces while preserving individual accountability.

Week
05

Data Interpretation and AI-Integrated Assessment

Understanding assignment and group-work analytics; assessment tools and best practices for interpreting AI-integrated performance evaluations — more efficient and scalable without sacrificing rigor.

Week
06

Optimize with AI — Capstone

Faculty revisit an existing activity or resource from their own course and redesign it using BoodleBox, leveraging AI to reshape, refine, and reimagine an assignment, project, or system in their curriculum.
